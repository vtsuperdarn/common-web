The Deep Space Climate Observatory, or DSCOVR mission monitors solar-wind and the Interplanetary Magnetic Field (IMF). DSCOVR will succeed NASA's Advanced Composition Explore's (ACE) role in supporting solar-wind alerts and warnings from the L1 orbit. The real time solar-wind data is provided by NOAA.
